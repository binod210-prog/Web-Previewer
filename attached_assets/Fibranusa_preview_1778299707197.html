Our Story

From Waste
to Wonder

Fibranusa was born from a simple observation: billions of banana sheaths are discarded each year, while the fashion industry struggles to find sustainable alternatives to animal leather.

Our patented process transforms these agricultural byproducts into a luxurious, durable material — without harming animals or the planet.

Sustainability

Our Commitment
to the Earth

Every decision we make is filtered through one question: is this good for people and planet?

85%
Less water
Our process conserves 85% of the water used in traditional leather tanning — saving millions of litres annually.
Zero
Harmful chemicals
No chromium, no formaldehyde. Every step uses food-safe, plant-based processes — safe for makers and wearers.
2M+
Sheaths saved
Banana sheaths that would have been burned or buried are now transformed into premium bio-leather materials.
100%
Biodegradable
At end of life, every Fibranusa product returns cleanly to the earth — no microplastics, no toxic residue.
200+
Farmers supported
We partner with over 200 banana farmers across Central Java, providing fair income from agricultural waste.

How We Do It

Sustainability in Every Step

From farm to finished product, every decision is made with the planet in mind.

🌱
Circular Economy
Agricultural waste transformed into luxury goods — nothing goes to landfill in our production process.
🤝
Fair Trade Principles
Every farmer and artisan in our supply chain is paid fairly. We believe sustainability starts with people.
📜
Transparent Supply Chain
From banana farm to finished product, we document and publish every step of our production journey.
🎨
Heritage Craft
We collaborate with local Javanese artisans to preserve traditional weaving and leatherwork techniques.
📦
Minimise Plastic Packaging
We are committed to minimising plastic in all packaging, using recycled paper, seed paper, and natural linen wherever possible.
🔋
Renewable Energy
Our production facility runs on 80% solar energy, reducing our operational carbon footprint significantly.
